Our Mission

At Journal Of Me, our mission is simple: Help you stay consistent with your journaling. You too know consistency is where the magic happens, but it’s also the hardest part when it comes to journaling. We believe that audio journaling is the easiest way to form a journaling habit. Our app is designed to make journaling so smooth that before you know it, you’ve formed a habit.

While we’re big fans of AI, we believe AI should only be used to make the process easier—not to take over. We’ll never do AI summaries or paraphrasing. This is your journal, your thoughts, and your space. Our logo — the scribble represents that your thoughts don't need to be perfect, your journal is a place where you should be unfiltered.
